Disc Valve (PRO-DV)

Supply / Exhaust Air Valve

Product Description

Disc Valve is an adjustable air terminal device used for supply and exhaust air applications in ventilation systems. The airflow is controlled by rotating the central disc to adjust the free area between disc and frame.

Key Features

  • Alternative to ceiling diffuser
  • Adjustable airflow control
  • Simple aesthetic design
  • Suitable for damp environments
  • Supply or exhaust use
  • Tight air sealing design
  • Low noise operation
  • Corrosion resistant coating
  • Suitable wall or ceiling installation
  • Easy maintenance
  • Aesthetic design for architectural use

Backdraft Damper (PRO-BDD)

Automatic Spring-Loaded Damper

Working Principle

Airflow present → blades open automatically

Airflow stops → spring closes blades

Result: Prevents reverse airflow and contamination

Construction & Materials

Housing Galvanized steel
Blade Type Butterfly folding blades
Blade Material Aluminium
Spring Stainless steel
Seal Rubber / Foam seal
Finish Black powder coated
Operation Automatic spring loaded

Applications

  • Inline duct fan systems
  • Bathroom exhaust
  • Kitchen ventilation
  • HVAC air distribution
  • Residential & commercial ventilation
