    HS-200P Mixed Flow Inline Fan

    The HS-200P Mixed Flow Inline Fan is engineered for high-performance ducted ventilation where strong airflow and higher static pressure are required. Utilizing advanced mixed flow technology, it delivers efficient air movement with low noise levels, even in long or complex duct systems. With dual-speed (HS/LS) operation and a durable, energy-efficient motor, the HS-200P is ideal for residential, commercial, and light industrial applications including offices, kitchens, corridors, and utility areas.
