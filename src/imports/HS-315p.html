    HS-315P Mixed Flow Inline Fan

    The HS-315P Mixed Flow Inline Fan is a powerful and efficient ventilation unit engineered for applications requiring high airflow capacity combined with strong static pressure performance. Utilizing advanced mixed flow technology, it merges the benefits of axial and centrifugal fans to deliver superior efficiency, stable airflow, and reduced noise levels. Designed for medium to large ventilation systems, the HS-315P is ideal for installations with long duct runs, multiple bends, or high system resistance. Its aerodynamic impeller ensures smooth air movement while maintaining excellent pressure characteristics across the operating range. The fan is equipped with dual speed operation (High Speed / Low Speed), allowing users to select the optimal balance between airflow performance and acoustic comfort. The inline cylindrical housing enables straightforward installation directly into circular ductwork, making it suitable for both new projects and retrofit applications.
