    MS-315M Backward Centrifugal Fan

    Designed for maximum airflow performance and high static pressure applications, the MS-315M delivers powerful ventilation for extensive ducted systems. Featuring a dynamically balanced backward-curved impeller, it ensures stable airflow, reduced vibration, and efficient energy consumption during continuous heavy-duty operation. Ideal for demanding commercial and industrial HVAC installations.
