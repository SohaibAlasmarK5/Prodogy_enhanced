    HS-125P Mixed Flow Inline Fan

    The HS-125 Mixed Flow Inline Fan is a powerful and efficient ventilation solution designed for residential and light commercial applications. By combining axial airflow with centrifugal pressure, it provides strong static pressure and stable airflow performance, even in extended duct systems. With low noise operation, an energy-efficient motor, and dual-speed (HS/LS) functionality, the HS-125 is ideal for bathrooms, kitchens, offices, and medium HVAC duct installations. Its durable construction ensures reliable long-term operation and simple maintenance.
