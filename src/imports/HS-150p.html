    HS-150P Mixed Flow Inline Fan

    The HS-150P Mixed Flow Inline Fan offers powerful airflow with high static pressure, making it ideal for medium ducted ventilation systems. Designed with mixed flow technology, it ensures efficient air movement, low noise levels, and reliable performance even over longer duct runs. Featuring dual-speed operation (HS/LS) and a robust motor, the HS-150P is suitable for residential, commercial, and light industrial applications such as bathrooms, kitchens, offices, and utility spaces.
